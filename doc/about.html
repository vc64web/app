Free Rom replacements

If you do not have legal access to the original Commodore Roms, you can run the emulator with the free Rom replacements that have been developed as part of the Mega65 project. The most recent versions of these Roms will be automatically loaded directly from the Mega65 site and can be installed with a single click in the Rom configuration panel.

The demo scene is great they surprise and amaze us every day with technical and artful new masterpieces for the Commodore C64 ... here are some of the most adored demos picked and hand selected by us.



VVVVVV playable demo

This is the playable demo from 2010 of a brilliant game by Terry Cavanagh. The great C64 port was done by Paul Koller.
If you like the demo, please have a look at the full version at https://thelettervsixtim.es.

Instructions:
On desktops press space to start, then use cursor keys to go left and right, use space bar to switch gravitation.
On touchscreens touch right game screen to start, then swipe in the left half of the canvas to move men, touch right half of screen to switch gravitation.

Wolfling

Lazycows mindblowing Wolfling draws you into a mystic world...
Ling awakes in Baron Baranov's dungeon. The baron fears all creatures of the night and Ling knows that her dark heritage is the reason for her imprisonment. Can she escape from the dungeon?

press LEFT and RIGHT to run, press DOWN to crouch, press UP to jump, hold UP to jump higher
press FIRE to punch, press FIRE while jumping for a kick,hold FIRE to throw daggers (if you have any)

while being a wolf
press LEFT and RIGHT to run, press DOWN for a roll attack, press UP to jump, hold UP to jump higher
press FIRE to bite, hold FIRE to morph back to human form

on desktop: directions with cursor keys, fire by pressing space
on touchscreen: directions by swiping on left half screen, fire by touching on the right half screen


(we added savepoint support with [s]ave and [l]oad buttons, just drag them into the offscreen in case you don't need them)

Moonspire II early preview demo

This is the playable freeware preview version with one level from 2017 of a brilliant game by Dušan Milivojević.
If you like the demo, please have a look at the full version at https://rgcddev.itch.io/moonspire.

Instructions:
On desktops press space to start, then use cursor keys to go left and right, use space bar to fire, use w key to choose weapon.
On touchscreens touch right game screen to start, then swipe in the left half of the canvas to move robot, touch right half of screen to fire, press weapon button to choose weapon.

Adventure

is it the first digital adventure ? This is Adventure by Adams (1979). Commodore 64 version by Brian Howarth.

press Y then N and then the Enter key
Now you’re in a forest. You can see loads of trees and you can go in any direction. GO EAST to a sunny meadow. There’s a dragon sleeping here so we better be quiet. There’s also a weird sign with something about mud. GO EAST again and you’re on the shore of a lake. You can see water and a golden fish. There’s also a rusty axe with a magic word on it. GET AXE and GO NORTH to a quick sand bog. You’re stuck now. A small statue can be found here. GET OX and SAY BUNYON to free yourself from the quicksand. You can now SWIM to the shore. Oops.. did you read the sign?
